Academic Programs

Science Program

Our Science program includes subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Students will gain in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience in various scientific fields.

  • Physics: Concepts and Practical Work
  • Chemistry: Laboratory Techniques and Theoretical Knowledge
  • Biology: Cell Biology, Genetics, and Ecology

Arts Program

Our Arts program covers subjects like Literature, History, and Fine Arts. This program is designed for students interested in humanities and creative expression.

  • Literature: Analysis and Critique of Classic and Modern Works
  • History: World and Local History Perspectives
  • Fine Arts: Painting, Sculpture, and Art History
  • Home Economics
