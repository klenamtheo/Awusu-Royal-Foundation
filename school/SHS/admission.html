Application Process

Welcome to the admissions page of AFIFE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL. Here you will find all the information you need to apply to our school.

The application process is straightforward and involves the following steps:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents as specified below.
  3. Attend an entrance interview (if applicable).
  4. Receive admission decision via email.
  5. Complete the enrollment process.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form.
  • Copy of birth certificate.
  • Copy of previous school records (transcripts or report cards).
  • Two passport-sized photographs.
  • Proof of residence.
  • Completed health form (if required).

Fees and Scholarships

Tuition Fees: Tuition fees are set annually and vary based on the grade level. Please contact our admissions office for the current fee structure.

Scholarships: We offer several scholarships based on academic performance and financial need. Applications for scholarships should be submitted along with the admission application.
